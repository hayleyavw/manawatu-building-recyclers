Whether you are building a new home or restoring a beautiful character home, we will have something to suit. All in stock materials are displayed at 86 Bourke St, Palmerston North and sorted for easy viewing and selection for our customers.

In our recycled range we are spezialising in a range of windows and doors. They are often, however, sold before we can even display them - so we can't guarantee how long specific items stay in stock. Our collection is always turning over and new material is added to our stock every day. There is bound to be something useful or of interest to you.

As well as recycled material we offer a range new French Doors, Modern & Traditional Windows as well as complete sets of Kitchens & Cupboard . These can be manufactured to your requirements. Our stocklist gives you all the information. However if you unsure please call we are very happy to help.

We have a delivery service. There may be an extra charge depending on distance, carrier and approximate time required.
